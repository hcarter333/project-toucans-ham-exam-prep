Privacy Policy — Extra Class Exam Tester2

Effective date: 2025-09-09

Who we are

This policy applies to the custom GPT named “Extra Class Exam Tester2,” published by Hamilton Carter (“we,” “us”).

What the GPT does

This GPT helps users study for the U.S. Amateur Radio Extra Class exam. It renders an interactive practice exam in Canvas and, when asked, explains concepts in plain English.

Data the GPT processes

  • User messages: Messages you type in ChatGPT are processed by OpenAI to generate responses. We do not receive or store your messages outside OpenAI’s platform.
  • External content: The GPT and its Canvas page may fetch publicly-available content from raw.githubusercontent.com (question pool and assets) and cdn.jsdelivr.net (Chart.js). These services receive standard technical information such as your IP address and user agent when content is requested.
  • Custom Actions (if enabled): The GPT may call an Action that fetches the latest question pool from GitHub. No personal information is intentionally sent in these requests.

Cookies & tracking

We do not set cookies or use trackers outside what is necessary to render the content from OpenAI, GitHub, or jsDelivr.

Data retention

We do not keep separate copies of your messages or personal data. Any retention is governed by OpenAI’s policies.

Your choices

  • You can refrain from using the optional Canvas features that load external assets.
  • You can clear Canvas content or stop using the GPT at any time.

Children

This GPT is intended for adults and older teens preparing for the Extra Class exam.
